Ambulance Route Optimiser






Ambulance route optimiser is a software that utilises Tkinter library in python to offer a captivating front end, in which the ambulance driver, or someone in emergency selects the starting and ending destination. Our software then utilises Traffic data and Djikastra's Algortithm to find the most optimised path between the two destinations.
Player Scouting Tool






Coded in Core Java, Player scouting tool is an extremely useful tool, which can be used by scouts to scout players they want for their team. Our software takes multitude of inputs and attributes the scouter wants for the potential player, and utilises the humongous dataset of football players, to find the best match.
Frogger Game





Frogger is a popular arcade game, where a frog needs to overcome multiple obsticles to reach the final destination. I coded my version of Frogger in MIPS Assembly language, where the frog has 3 lives before the game gets over. I have introduced obstacles like water and fire, and safe zones like wood log and platform.
